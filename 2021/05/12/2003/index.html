    Text 3

    单词 / 词组

    1. heighten v. 提高
    2. ton-mile 吨-英里 货运单位
    3. freight n. 货运，货物 v. 运输，充满
    4. major rail carriers 主要货运公司
    5. shipper n. 托运人，货运公司
    6. have…by the throat 扼住咽喉，完全控制
    7. surge n./v. 汹涌，上升
    8. Wall Street 华尔街
    9. capital n. 资金
    10. acquire v. 收购；得到

    句子

    1. The rail industry as a whole, despite its brightening fortunes, still doesn’t earn enough to cover the cost of the capital it must to keep up with its surging traffic.
    2. Yet railroads continue to borrow billons to acquire one another, with Wall Street cheering them on.
    3. Consider the $10.2 billon bid by Norfolk Southern and CSX to acquire Conrail this year. Conrail’s net railway operating income in 1996 was just $427 million, less than half of the carrying costs of the transaction.

    Text 4

    单词 / 词组

    1. it is said 据说
    2. pressing a. 难以推却的；紧急的
    3. small wonder 不足为奇
    4. failing hips 损坏的髋骨
    5. clinical a. 临床的；诊所的
    6. cataract n. 白内障
    7. surgical procedure 外科手术 = surgery
    8. disintegrate v. 解体，衰弱
    9. perish v. 死亡，毁灭
    10. far beyond 远超出
    11. go that far 极端
    12. dazzlingly ad. 璀璨地，耀眼地
    13. former surgeon general 前卫生部长
    14. humble a. 普通的；谦虚的；低贱的
    15. reserved a. 矜持的，内向的；预约的
    16. reserved consent 有保留的赞同

    句子

    1. But not even a great health care system can cure death —- and our failure to confront that reality now threatens this greatness of ours.
    2. Shielded by third-party payers from the cost of ours, we demand everything that can possibly done for us,even if it’s useless.
    3. Anyone can see this trend is unsustainable. Yet few seem willing to try to reverse it.
    4. As a nation, we may be overfunding the quest for the unlikely cures while underfunding research on humbler therapies that could improve people’s lives.
    5. These leaders are the living proof that prevention works and that we can manage the health problems that come naturally with age.
